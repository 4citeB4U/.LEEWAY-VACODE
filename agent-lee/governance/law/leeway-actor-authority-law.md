# LeeWay Actor Authority Law

Every participant in LeeWay must have an actor ID before it can influence governed work.

LLMs are cognitive equipment. Agents are governed actors. Humans retain authority over destructive, foundational, external, and data-affecting actions.

## Actor ID Format

`LEEWAY_ACTOR::<CLASS>::<POSITION>::<INSTANCE>`

## Authority Law

- Human owners may approve foundational law changes.
- LeeWay agents may act only through registered transactions, gates, evidence, and receipts.
- LLM outputs are advisory until accepted by a LeeWay-governed agent transaction.
- Scripts and gates must identify their authority profile before changing evidence or package state.
- External or untrusted actors must be quarantined until traceability is established.
